FAQ's

If there is additional information you would like to have in the faq's make usre to head over to Feedback and let us know.

  1. What type of game is it?

    It is a matching game that you can ctest your skills against a computer. The aim is to get the same image that the computer picks.

  2. How can I win the game?

    You can win if you manage to get 5 matches in 10 turns.

  3. How do i play the game?

    To play you first click the lets go button here. Then once your in the game area you pick any of the 6 buttons at the bottom. Once you make your selection the computer will make a selection.

  4. How do i reset the game?

    When your in the game area if you click on the red reset button at the bottom of the screen it will reset the game for you.

  5. How do i find the list?

    Click on the list tab in the nav bar here

  6. How do i add an item to the list?

    Type your text into the input section. Once it is typed you can click the plus button on the right of the text field to create a line item.

  7. How do i mark an item as complete on the list?

    On the line item you just created if you click the green check button it will mark it as complete and fade it out.

  8. How do i delete an item from the list?

    On the line item you just created if you click the red x button it will delete the line item from the list.

  9. How do i submit feedback?

    If you click on the envalope icon in the footer it will take you to the feedback page.
